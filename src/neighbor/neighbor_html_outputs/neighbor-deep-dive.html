The Thornock Ranch actively seeks agricultural workers for cattle and hay operations (ranchworldads.com), confirming its status as an active agricultural entity rather than a passive speculative investment. 22200340002000, 22200130002000 Willis Land Wyoming LLC llc Willis Land Wyoming LLC is a Wyoming limited liability company registered to Jordon Willis of Cokeville, Wyoming (wyobiz.wyo.gov). Contrary to reports of mystery ownership, the entity is the land-holding arm of the Willis Ranch, a multi-generational family operation established in the area since the late 1960s (westernagcredit.com). The ranch is operated by Linda Willis and her sons James, Jed, and Jordon, running approximately 1,800 head of cattle and farming malt barley and hay (westernagcredit.com). The family utilizes precision agriculture technology to manage crop damage and improve yields (stotzequipment.com). While the entity holds land in the project vicinity, it is a functional agricultural business rather than a shell company for speculative investment. Medium agriculture Agricultural productivity, Family legacy. Contact Jordon Willis directly; respect the family's long-standing stewardship of the land. Frame any proposals as complementary to their advanced agricultural practices and cattle operations rather than a buyout. H
